History of our hotels

OTIUM subsidiaries are operating in Turkey, Russia, Poland, Ukraine, Belarus, Georgia, Thailand, Egypt, UAE, Spain and Greece. It has positioned itself in many countries under the trademarks of Otium Travel (Russia, Turkey, Ukraine, Belarus, Poland, Georgia, Russia, Turkey, Poland, Egypt, Thailand, Spain, UAE and Greece). Through expansion, OTIUM HOTELS is entering into new markets and destinations, making progress without compromising on quality and customer satisfaction. The number of customers served by the Group in 2017 was 1 million 873 thousand, whereas it reached to 2 million 400 thousand in 2019 with an increase of over 30%, including the package tours.
